## A Summit on Software Architecture in the Age of AI

Discover the seamless integration of Software Architecture and Artificial Intelligence at our upcoming event. Explore the intricate bonds that elevate software systems to new heights, where architectural principles converge with AI brilliance. Join us for an enlightening journey where innovation and architecture unite in a symphony of technological excellence.

- **+20** sponsors
- **+15** speakers
- **+35** countries
- **+600** attendees

## Practical Talks and Hands-on Workshops

GSAS (Global Software Architecture Summit) is a 3-days summit which aims to attract and connect software architecture experts from all over the world as well as all those interested in building working software to improve their skills, share knowledge, and connect.

## GSAS 2024 Insights

GSAS 2024 will cover architectural patterns, DDD, Event Storming, Modularity, Micro-frontends, Serverless, Functional Declarative Design, Architectural Decisions, Green Software, real-world examples, production-ready architectural solutions among others.

- Architectural Patterns
- Event Storming
- Domain-Driven Design (DDD)
- Functional Declarative Design
- Architectura Decisions
- Green Software
