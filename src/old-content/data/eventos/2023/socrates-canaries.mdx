## About the event

Socrates is about PEOPLE, about ACTION, about FUN

Learn and share new ideas, techniques, languages, and frameworks through coding dojos, pair programming, mob programming, discussion groups, presentations, and informal conversations. As an unconference itself, there is no agenda and there are no speakers before the event starts. Proposals are presented and voted on by the participants at the venue.

The retreat will be run as a non-profit, low-cost event. The tickets will cover accommodation, meals (as specified below), and meeting rooms.

Socrates is quite an experience, spending time with so many interesting, passionate and nice people. Get ready for some fun!

If you want to know more about the history of Socrates The Conference, take a look at <a target="_blank" href="https://www.socrates-conference.de/home">this</a>.

For any query please do not hesitate to contact us through <a target="_blank" href="mailto:socracan@gmail.com">socracan@gmail.com</a>.
