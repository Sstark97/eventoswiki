## The event

Experience the brilliance of functional programming at Lambda World in Cadiz, Spain.

Join us from October 2nd to 4th, 2024, to connect with our (dis)functional family, dive into FP trends, and soak up the coastal charm of this must-attend event!

## Main Topics

- AI & ML with FP
- Software Architecture & System Design
- FP in Web Development
- Data with FP
- Computation in functional technologies
- Emerging FP Tools & Trends

## What we expect for 2024

- **600+** Attendees from all over

- **16+** Hours of functional goodness

- **24+** Speakers from around the globe

- **2 Parallel Tracks** plus an entertainment lounge
