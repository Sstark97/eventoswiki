## The Growth Conference: 3 Days Full of Growth

Join +500 Growth-minded peers in Madrid (Spain) for a full day of inspiring keynotes and new connections at the premier Growth conference.

- **500+** Attendees
- **40+** Speakers
- **30+** Sessions

## Meet Hacking Growth's father

Sean Ellis is the architect behind the explosive growth of industry titans such as **Dropbox, Eventbrite, Lookout, and LogMeIn**, orchestrating go-to-market strategies that propelled these companies towards billion-dollar valuations.

He will be giving a **Masterclass** and a **Workshop** at **Product Hackers Conference 2024**.
