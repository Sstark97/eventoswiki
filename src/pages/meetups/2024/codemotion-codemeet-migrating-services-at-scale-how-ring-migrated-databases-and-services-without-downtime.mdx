## Technology brings us together!

This October 2nd, join us for our exciting meetup.

Discover how Ring's teams in Madrid transformed their infrastructure, moving from a monolithic setup to agile, 2-pizza teams managing their own services and improving operational processes in the new era of distributed systems.

Additionally, enter a raffle and take home an Amazon souvenir. :)

## Why Attend?

➡️ Connection: Meet like-minded professionals who share a passion for technology and innovation. Expand your network and connect with new communities.

➡️ Innovation: Learn how centralizing infrastructure impacts development and how distributed systems can drive efficiency and growth.

➡️ Inspiration: Hear from industry leaders about their journey in scaling services and overcoming challenges in a large organization.

➡️ Networking: Connect with experts and peers to exchange ideas and insights on managing distributed systems.
