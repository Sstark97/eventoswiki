> This time we have been collaborating with Mainmatter to prepare this special event called Rust for the web. We will have two talks and a final panel.

## TALK 1: Excitement of Football, Reliability of Rust by [Matilda Smeds](https://www.linkedin.com/in/matildasmeds/)

**Abstract**: In this talk Matilda explores architectural choices, stack, and the rollercoaster of experiences along the way, of developing a backend of a football mobile game. Join in to hear about the twists, turns and unexpected delights of building a distributed backend with Rust.

**Bio**: [Matilda Smeds](https://www.linkedin.com/in/matildasmeds/) is a seasoned software engineer, who has spent the last three years in the gaming industry. In her current role at [Matchday](https://matchday.com/), she leverages Rust to develop a backend for a football mobile game.

**Abstract**: To be announced.

**Bio**: [Gleb Pomykalov](https://www.linkedin.com/in/glebpom/) is an Engineering Director at [Workato](https://www.workato.com/).

## FINAL PANEL

A panel with both speakers will follow the talks. [Luca Palmieri](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luca-palmieri/) will be moderating the discussion.

**Moderator bio**: [Luca Palmieri](https://www.linkedin.com/in/luca-palmieri/) works as a Principal Engineering Consultant at [Mainmatter](https://mainmatter.com/). He has been part of the Rust community since 2018 and is best known as the author of “Zero to Production in Rust”, the go-to book for backend development in Rust.
