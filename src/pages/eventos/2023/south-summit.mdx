## THE MOST VALUABLE NETWORK FOR ENTREPRENEURSHIP

Welcome to the annual gathering of the key players in the entrepreneurial ecosystem in Madrid. Connect with top minds in innovation from all over the world.

## WHY SOUTH SUMMIT?

### Startups

- Get to share your project with international investors attending
- Learn from the best. CEOs from unicorns sharing their expertise
- Connect and meet with thousands of potential clients

### Corporations

- Expand your Open Innovation initiatives with hundreds of new projects
- Access top-tier talent with our networking opportunities
- Meet other corporates and investors. Share your expertise and learn from theirs

### Investors

- Grow your portfolio with super disruptive startups attending
- Scout for new talent from the top profiles attending
- Share knowledge and experiences with other international investors
