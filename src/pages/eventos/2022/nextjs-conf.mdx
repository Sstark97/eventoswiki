## Learn to build better and scale faster

We’re excited to announce the third annual Next.js Conf on October 25, 2022. <a target="_blank" href="https://nextjs.org/conf">Claim your ticket now</a>.

Since creating Next.js (on October 25, 2016) as a server-rendered framework for building React applications, it has evolved to become the industry’s go-to fullstack React framework for the Web, with over 200 million downloads.

Today we also <a target="_blank" href="https://twitter.com/vercel/status/1565735933512626176?s=20&t=sOk-oqlx0GG5K5GGZ04I2">unveiled</a> the new logo. Similar to the Next.js Core Team at Vercel, it's bolder and stronger than ever. In celebration of six years of Next.js, this team will unveil some of our largest improvements at Next.js Conf.

Join us virtually, along with some of the brightest minds in the React ecosystem, for a conference that will give you the tools you need to build better and scale faster.

### Want to tell your story? Be a speaker.

This year's presentations will be our best yet, starting with the keynote by Guillermo Rauch (Vercel CEO & co-creator of Next.js). Join the speaker lineup by submitting a proposal.
